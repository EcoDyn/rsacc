Data Description:

This global reference dataset is produced from very high resolution commercial remote sensing data, circa 2010, collected for a stratified random sample of 500 sites. The sampling design supports global estimates of agreement with four major land cover classes: Tree, Water, Barren and Other Vegetation. Thematic rasters at 2 meter resolution, map 7 classes (5 land cover, 2 non-land cover). Sampling design and sample density  are designed for assessment of global land cover data but are valid at continental scale with reduced confidence levels.
